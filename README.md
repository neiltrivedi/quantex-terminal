# Quantex Terminal

**A free financial research platform built by Neil Trivedi.**

Quantex Terminal helps students and beginner investors research any stock in
seconds. It analyzes a company across six factors and combines them into a single
AI-enhanced research signal, so you can understand a stock without a finance
degree or an expensive subscription.

**Live platform:** [quantexterminal.com](https://quantexterminal.com)

---

## What it does

Most stock research tools are either too expensive or too complicated for someone
just starting out. Quantex Terminal was built to fix that. You search a ticker,
and the platform turns a company's fundamentals into something you can actually
understand and act on.

- **6-Factor Signal:** Every stock is scored across six distinct factors that
  combine into one AI-enhanced research signal with a clear verdict.
- **Real market data:** Fundamentals and pricing come from public market data
  sources.
- **Beginner-first design:** Everything lives on one clean screen. No jargon
  walls, no clutter.
- **Free to use:** No paywall.

## Who it's for

Students, beginner investors, and anyone who has ever found stock research
intimidating. If you can use a search bar, you can research a company with Quantex.

## The 6-factor signal

The core of Quantex Terminal is its quantitative signal. Instead of dumping raw
numbers on you, the platform evaluates a stock across six research factors, weighs
them, and produces a single composite signal (for example, a "Hold / Slight Buy"
verdict with a score). The goal is not to tell you what to buy. It is to give you
a clear, honest, research-based starting point so you can make your own decision.

> Quantex Terminal is for educational purposes and is not financial advice.

## Tech stack

- **Backend:** Python / Flask
- **Data:** Public market data sources (fundamentals and pricing)
- **Analytics:** PostHog (privacy-friendly, anonymous usage analytics)
